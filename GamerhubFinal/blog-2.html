Smite Battleground of the Gods
If You Looking For A Fun Game Playing As Your Favorite God And Goddess Like Zeus, Hades, Anubis, Loki, Pele, And So Much More. Trust Me Smite Is Defenly The  Game For You. When I First Playing This Game, It Was Confusing, But Soon You Get The Hang Of This Game And Figure Out The Items That Go With Each God This Game Get Really Fun. Especially With Friends.
So Smite Is Consider A Moba. So, What Do That Mean Its Means Its A 5v5 Battle So You And 4 Others Fighting Against Another Team, The Object Is To Destroy Their First 3 Towers (weakest) Then Destroy The Next 3 Towers (second Weakest), After That You Have To Destroy Their 3 Phoenix (really Strong), And Finally You Must Kill You Oppennt Titan (strong). The First Team To Do This Win The Game. Smite Is A Team Battle, So You Need Sterigy And And Teamwork To Get To The Titan.
