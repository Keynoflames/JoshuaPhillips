My Hero Academia Is the Best anime of 2021!
My Hero Academia First Aired In North America In 2019, And Which Was Extremely Exciting For Anime Because Its Introduce Superheros
in Anime. With Four Season In My Hero Showed Over 50 Different Characters, And Each And Every Characters Show Characters Development. Basically, You See Each Hero And Villians Improving Their Abilities And Powers As The Seasons Continue On. Which Is Really Impressive Because So Many Animes That The Createors Forget About The Side Characters And They Just Die Off Or Become Incredibly Weak Comepared To The Main Characters With The Show.
The Plots Of The Stories Is Also Amazing. They Made It Feel Exactly Like A Everyday Hero Comic Where Sometimes The Heros Win And Sometimes They Lose, And The Villains Get Away. It's Definitely A Cool Twist Because When The Heroes Are In A Fierce Battle And In The Heat Of The Moment, The Hero Can Use Their Strongest Attack, Then The Villain Just Counter It, And Boom That Can Be The Last Time You Might See The Hero. It's Definitely Keep You On Your Toes.
